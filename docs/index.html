What is this and why give a fuck?

It's
  • thrifty
  • creative
  • radical
  • and fun!
Status quo is founded on info asymmetry: Few can master a black art, so we must attempt to convey our ideas through the masters, and pay them for the privilege. But I suspect it's actually pretty accessible and you don't have to achieve "mastery" in order to make a cool site. Thanks to idealism of the Web ecosystem, the barrier is always getting lower... And fun because of virtually limitless possibilities, including to share and remix freely.
